What is CopperOS?

CopperOS is a small experimental x86 OS for learning and demos.

How do I try it?

Use the Try it button on the homepage to attempt running the OS in your browser (if supported), or download the ISO and run it in QEMU/VirtualBox.

Is it safe for production?

No. Not for production. It's experimental and may be unstable.
